Back story

We have been in the jewelry business since 1954 (60 years of experience).

About the store

We sell beautiful estate and fine jewelry, and we will buy your old gold, silver, gemstones and other valuables. Our friendly, honest, no-pressure approach will ensure that youll be comfortable in our store whether youre looking to buy or sell. We currently have 2 showrooms at our beautiful 7th street location. We have a huge selection of rare and collectible coins in our coin gallery and we also carry items collectors will need to grow and showcase their current collection. Our second showroom consists of estate and new jewelry. We sell all types of jewelry from wedding sets to Rolex watches. We also have a very popular $3/gram section of collectible indian and silver jewelry.
