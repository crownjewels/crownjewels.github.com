About

Crown Jewels and Coin pays the highest market values for coins and jewelry. We have been in the jewelry business since 1974.

We sell beautiful estate and fine jewelry, and we will buy your old gold, silver, gemstones and other valuables. Our friendly, honest, no-pressure approach will ensure that you’ll be comfortable in our store whether you’re looking to buy or sell. We currently have 2 showrooms at our beautiful 7th street location. We have a huge selection of rare and collectible coins in our coin gallery and we also carry items collectors will need to grow and showcase their current collection. Our second showroom consists of estate and new jewelry. We sell all types of jewelry from wedding sets to Rolex watches. We also have a very popular $3/gram section of collectible indian and silver jewelry.


Let our expert and friendly staff find you that special piece of jewelry that you are looking for, our prices are the best in the valley!
