Silver

If you're unsure whether or not their item is silver bring it in and we will test it and let you know the results.

Crown Jewels and Coin purchases all Silver items. If it is stamped Sterling Silver or 925 we will purchase it. We will carefully examine your pieces and provide you with a thorough explanation of our value assessment. Our assessments will always reflect fair market value, and we will not pressure you to sell. We want your experience with us to be as comfortable and stress-free as possible.
